##### Laplace Transform

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{V_1(s)}{R} = -CsV_2(s)
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\therefore \frac{V_2(s)}{V_1(s)} = - \frac{1}{RCs}
\end{aligned}
$$
